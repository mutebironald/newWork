# 10 Launch and Growth Plan

## Launch objective
Acquire pilot users, process real revenue, and produce evidence of paid work episodes within the hackathon period.

## Primary launch path
Run one to three small cohorts through existing community channels.

Recommended pilot partners:
- Youth employment groups
- University entrepreneurship clubs
- Churches or community groups
- Women’s economic empowerment groups
- Local incubators
- Diaspora-funded community programs

## Direct user acquisition
Use lightweight campaigns:
- Turn your skills into paid work
- Help local merchants and earn
- Start as a NewWork Field Agent
- Create your first merchant service offer

## Merchant acquisition
Agents recruit merchants directly through:
- Personal networks
- WhatsApp groups
- Local markets
- Family businesses
- Churches or community centers
- Small shops and food vendors

## Launch funnel

1. Partner or campaign invites agents.
2. Agent completes skill scan.
3. Agent selects service offer.
4. Agent contacts merchants.
5. Agent completes first service.
6. Merchant pays.
7. Agent uploads proof.
8. Admin exports impact report.

## Flow video brief

### Video title
NewWork: Turning Skills Into Paid Work

### Video structure

Scene 1: The problem
A young person has skills, a phone, and local knowledge, but no clear path to income. Nearby merchants need help organizing records, reaching customers, and promoting products.

Scene 2: The product
NewWork helps the young person become a field agent. AI identifies their skills and recommends practical merchant services.

Scene 3: The work
The agent helps a merchant digitize receipts, create a WhatsApp catalog, and send customer follow-ups.

Scene 4: The proof
The merchant pays. The agent logs the paid work episode and uploads proof. The program dashboard updates.

Scene 5: The impact
NewWork shows real revenue, AI workflows, verified paid work episodes, and proof-of-work.

### Visual style
- Subdued navy and cream
- Realistic market and small-business scenes
- Human, practical, grounded
- No neon colors
- No futuristic clichés

## Partner pitch

NewWork helps youth employment and entrepreneurship programs prove income outcomes. Participants become AI-assisted field agents who provide practical services to informal merchants. The platform tracks skill scans, offers launched, merchants contacted, paid work episodes, agent income, proof uploads, and verification status.

## Sales motion

### Step 1: Founder-led pilot outreach
Send direct message to program leaders and community partners.

### Step 2: 30-minute pilot call
Walk through the dashboard and pilot design.

### Step 3: Small cohort launch
Start with 10 to 25 participants.

### Step 4: Evidence report
Deliver cohort report after 2 to 4 weeks.

### Step 5: Paid renewal
Convert to Cohort Pack or Partner Pilot.
